The [Lower
attic](/web/20191005050925/http://cantorsattic.info/Lower_attic "Lower attic")
contains countable infinite ordinals which can be proven to exist in
[ZFC](/web/20191005050925/http://cantorsattic.info/ZFC "ZFC").
